Help center

FAQ, guides, and announcements for OPMM

These address how this OPMM app routes data, filters, and notices—not generic "how to use a browser" tips. Use the Guide tab for full walkthroughs.

For many office-linked accounts the app’s default entry is a full fiscal-year matrix (all quarters, all development areas) so you land on live KPIs immediately. Superusers without extra URL parameters are routed the same way. If you need a single quarter, pick it in the viewer’s Period strip once you arrive.

Administrators can post globally or to selected offices only. Office-scoped posts are delivered only to accounts that resolve to that office in this system (your profile is matched to an Office record). If you expected the same notice, confirm with your admin that your account is tied to the correct office—not only your display name on the chip.

The viewer only enables quarters that actually have ingested performance rows for the fiscal year you selected. If ingestion never included that quarter, the tab stays disabled so you are not shown an empty matrix by mistake. Use All or another FY from the year dropdown if the quarter you need is missing.

The app reloads the viewer via HTMX and keeps your current fiscal year, quarter, office (when applicable), and development area from the URL—it only swaps in the focused indicator. That is intentional so you do not lose context when jumping between KPIs.

Variance reflects the monitor’s quantitative or textual “variance vs target” column. The status pill also considers explicit accomplishment language from the upload (for example narrative MET/UNMET) and inferred values when numbers are missing. When they differ, trust the pill for rollups and open the row detail modal to see both the narrative and the numbers side by side.

Clear office data (when shown) deletes ingested indicators and scores for your office only so you can re-upload monitors without touching other offices. A full reset is a superuser maintenance action that can wipe the entire strategic dataset—those controls are gated and should be rare. Check the Activity Log after either operation.

The export is a wide LPC workbook for the whole fiscal year (all quarters and development areas) so planners can work offline in one file. The on-screen matrix may still be narrowed to one quarter or area. The Office filter continues to apply to the export when you have one selected—so the file is “full FY for that office,” not necessarily identical to the current table view.

The badge counts active announcements you have not acknowledged yet in this app. Reading the drawer or dismissing the viewer toast marks the visible posts as read. If an administrator publishes a new active notice afterward, the unread count returns—that is expected, not a bug.

Open the sections below for fuller explanations. Everything here matches how the app behaves in your browser.

The search box finds text in your Outcome, Strategy, PAP, and indicator labels. Each suggestion shows where the match fits so you can confirm the right row before you open it.

  • Click a result to go to the Performance viewer with that KPI in focus. If you are already on the viewer, your fiscal year, quarter, office, and development-area filters stay applied.
  • Try a few distinctive words from the matrix (for example part of a strategy sentence or a PAP name) if the indicator title alone is too generic.
Esc closes the suggestion list and moves focus out of the search field. Click outside the search box to dismiss suggestions without clearing what you typed.

The Indicator matrix groups rows by outcome (color band), then strategy and PAP. Colored edge lines mark the hierarchy columns; the indicator column can show a thin accent for Met / Unmet / Pending.

  • Use FY, Period (all quarters or Q1–Q4), and Development area to narrow the table. Superusers can also pick an Office or combine offices.
  • Click any matrix row to open a detail panel with targets, accomplishments, variance, and remarks for that KPI.
  • Use Previous / Next at the bottom when the matrix is paginated.
  • Use Export LPC Excel in the matrix toolbar for a wide spreadsheet (full fiscal year, all quarters and development areas; the office filter still applies when you have one selected).
  • Use the bell beside your account for the announcements drawer; the red badge is your unread count until you open the drawer or dismiss the viewer toast.
  • Active notices may pop in as a large toast for about ten seconds — use × or Esc to dismiss early.

Open Dashboard in the left sidebar for the office overview. Analytics offers charts and summaries built from the same ingested performance data.

  • Superusers opening the dashboard without extra query parameters are sent to a full fiscal-year overview in the viewer by default.

Data Ingestion is where approved monitor files (for example Word or Excel OPMM layouts) are uploaded and parsed into indicators and scores.

  • Activity Log records uploads, backups, resets, and other system actions so you can audit who did what and when.

A thin cardinal loading bar may appear at the top of the window when the app fetches a new screen (for example HTMX updates to the dashboard or viewer). Wait for it to finish before clicking again if a request feels slow.

Your profile chip opens quick actions. Use the bell beside your account for announcements (same posts as the Announcements tab here). This Help center opens from your account menu; superusers also see User management and Post announcements. Use Sign out on shared computers.
